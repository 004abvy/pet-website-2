Our Passion for Pets

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional care and products for your beloved companions

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by lifelong animal lover John Smith, Paws & Claws began as a small neighborhood pet supply store with a simple mission: to help pet owners provide the best care for their furry, feathered, and aquatic friends.

What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted destination for pet owners throughout the region. We've expanded our offerings to include not only premium pet foods and supplies but also professional grooming, veterinary services, and educational resources.

Today, we continue to uphold our founding principles of quality, compassion, and education for every pet parent. Our team of dedicated animal experts is here to help you navigate the journey of pet ownership with confidence and joy.
