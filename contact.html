Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

Do you offer emergency veterinary services?

Yes, we have an on-call veterinarian for emergencies during business hours. For after-hours emergencies, we recommend contacting the 24/7 Animal Emergency Clinic at (555) 789-0123.

What are your delivery options?

We offer free in-store pickup, local delivery within 10 miles for $5, and standard shipping nationwide. Express shipping is available for an additional fee.

How do I schedule a grooming appointment?

You can schedule grooming appointments online through our portal, by calling us directly, or by visiting our store. We recommend booking at least one week in advance.

What is your return policy?

Unopened products in original packaging can be returned within 30 days with receipt. For pet food returns, we require the product to be at least 3/4 full. Perishable and prescription items are final sale.
